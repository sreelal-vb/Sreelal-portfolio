About Me

I am a dedicated Hindu Priest specializing in wedding ceremonies, griha pravesh (housewarming poojas), and all traditional Hindu rituals. I responsibly arrange all pooja items for every event, so families can relax and focus on the sacred moments without rushing for materials.

I regularly work with leading wedding event management teams like Shaadi Wedding, Happiness Project, Maritus Events, Beyond The Décor to ensure seamless and spiritually fulfilling ceremonies.

I also have experience handling international and inter-state clients. Whether it is a marriage between families from India and other countries, or a wedding between different Indian states and traditions, I coordinate the rituals smoothly while respecting cultural and regional customs.

My goal is to provide authentic, peaceful, and well-organized pooja services for every sacred occasion — no matter where the families come from.
